Molding and casting project

Below you will find a detailed description of the project process.

Proceed Back To Front Page

In this project the objective was to make a mold for a part and then casting that mold with epoxy meterial. 3D printing can take alot of time and making a mold for a massevly produced part can cut the munufacturing time of that part and the whole product by alot.

The part I decided to make a mold of and cast is the x carriage for the hotend. This item is attached to the hotend holder that the hotend is mounted to. Through the sides it is connection to two horizontal smooth rods through bearings that are mounted in side of the two 15,2 mm cylindrical cuts.

Go to insert > features > cavity.

the cylindar shapes in the part created a problem. They made a "roof" where the part is supposed to be pulled out of the mold.

I modified the part so it would be possible to pull the part out of the mold.
